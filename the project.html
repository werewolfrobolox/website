    What is Harassment (Cyberbullying)?


    In cyberbullying, harassment refers to the persistent and unwanted behaviour that is intended to harm, threaten, or intimidate someone.

    This includes an act of using digital platforms or communication tools to intimidate, threaten, humiliate, or cause distress to an specific individual or a group.

    What are some examples of harassment?

    • Repeatedly sending offensive, rude, and insulting messages to someone via SMS, social media accounts, message board.
    • Threatening someone over the internet, including threats of physical harm, violence, or intimidation
