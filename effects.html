Preventing More Cases

However, There is something we can do to stop online harassment

  • Education/Awareness
    • Learning more about online safety and share that knowledge with others while discussing the importance of respectful behavior online would show an significant impact.
  • Mindful Sharing
    • Think twice before sharing personal information, opinions, or photos online. Once shared, it can be challenging to retract while avoid engaging in controversial discussions or sharing polarizing content that might attract negative attention.
  • Collaboration and Advocacy
    • Collaborate with platforms, advocacy groups, and policymakers to create and enforce regulations that combat online harassment effectively and advocating a safer online spaces and support initiatives that promote digital civility.
