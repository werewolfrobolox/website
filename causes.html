Effects Of Online Harassment

Emotional Impact

Harassment online can cause a range of emotions, including fear, anxiety, anger, and distress. It can lead to feelings of helplessness, humiliation, and frustration.

Mental Health Issue

Victims of online harassment may experience mental health issues such as depression, anxiety disorders, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and in severe cases, suicidal thoughts.

Social Isolation

Being harassed online might lead to victims withdrawing from social activities, avoiding certain online platforms, or even disconnecting from friends and family to protect themselves.

Cyberbullying Trauma

In cases of cyberbullying, particularly among younger individuals, the trauma experienced can affect academic performance, social interactions, and overall well-being.

Legal Issue

In extreme cases, online harassment may result in legal complications. However, navigating legal recourse for online harassment can be challenging due to varying laws and difficulties in identifying perpetrators.

Financial Consequences

Harassment may lead to financial loss if it affects someone's professional life, job, or business.
